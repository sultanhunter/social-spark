# Carousel Hook Strategy Playbook

This playbook turns high-performing Instagram carousel behavior into a repeatable system.

## 1) Open with Pattern-Breaking Friction

- Use an unexpected first-line hook that creates safe tension.
- Combine a familiar topic with a surprising verb or frame.
- Goal: force a scroll stop through curiosity, not clickbait.

Example pattern:

- `How to [unexpected action] for [familiar goal]`

## 2) Make Slide 2 a Second Hook

- Treat slide 2 as another entry point, not filler.
- Instagram can show slide 2 to people who already saw slide 1.
- Reuse a proven angle or stat-heavy claim that already worked.

Rule:

- Slide 2 must be independently strong enough to earn a swipe.

## 3) Pace Reading with Rhythm

- Alternate dense and lighter slides.
- Sequence example: dense -> light -> dense -> light.
- Prevent reader fatigue and increase completion rate.

Design principle:

- Think rhythm, not information dumping.

## 4) Use CAPS LOCK Intentionally

- Use caps only for attention-critical words.
- Treat caps like a highlighter, not a default style.
- A/B test cap placement on hook words and CTA words.

## 5) Write How You Actually Talk

- Read every line out loud.
- If you would not say it in real conversation, rewrite it.
- Remove robotic phrasing and abstract filler.

Voice target:

- Should feel like a practical voice note, not a textbook.

## 6) Lean into Density + Bullets

- Heavy value slides drive saves and shares.
- Bullets make dense insight skimmable and actionable.
- Use newsletter-style structure for clarity.

Format rule:

- Every dense slide should have clear bullets or numbered steps.

## 7) Use Negative Space

- Leave intentional blank space around text blocks.
- Improves readability and reduces cognitive overload.
- Makes slides feel premium and structured.

Layout rule:

- Do not crowd text against all edges.

## 8) Add Constructive Controversy

- Challenge passive or mainstream assumptions in your niche.
- Use respectful disagreement and conviction.
- Trigger discussion without sacrificing trust.

Guardrail:

- Be bold, not reckless.

## 9) Multiply Winning Angles

- Do not abandon a winning idea after one post.
- Create multiple entry points from the same core theme.
- Build momentum with thematic series.

Execution framework:

- Same theme, different hook.
- Same promise, different audience context.
- Same insight, different format (carousel, reel, caption thread).

## Slide Construction Checklist

- Slide 1 = primary hook (pattern break).
- Slide 2 = second hook (standalone strength).
- Slides 3-7 = value delivery with rhythmic pacing.
- Slide 8+ = synthesis, CTA, and next-angle seed.
- Maintain voice consistency, negative space, and selective caps.

## Muslimah Pro Adaptation Notes

- Keep content practical, faith-aligned, and respectful.
- Focus on Muslim women’s real-life period/pregnancy/lifestyle challenges.
- Prioritize actionable guidance, clear benefits, and compassionate tone.
- For generated images: use English-only text in artwork (no Arabic glyphs).

## Template-First Slide Format (Recommended)

- Use a fixed 4:5 slide canvas with a hyperrealistic modest hijabi woman background.
- Keep text in a top overlay block with generous margins.
- Avoid long text blocks inside the image.

Per-slide text limits:

- `overlayTitle`: max 8 words
- `overlayLines`: 0-2 lines, each max 10 words

Why this format works:

- Reduces model typography glitches.
- Prevents cut-off text near edges.
- Keeps readability high on mobile.
- Preserves hook rhythm while maintaining aesthetic consistency.
